## Thesis Statement
Nuclear power is experiencing its most important policy and commercial renaissance since the 1970s — driven by an unlikely coalition of tech companies desperate for 24/7 carbon-free power, defense hawks prioritizing energy security, and climate activists who've accepted that intermittent renewables cannot carry baseload alone. Microsoft restarted Three Mile Island (Constellation). Amazon, Google, and Meta are all signing nuclear PPAs. NRC certified the first SMR design (NuScale) in 2022. Uranium is in structural deficit. This is a multi-decade infrastructure investment cycle, not a speculative bubble.

## Core Logic
1. **AI data centers need 24/7 power that wind/solar can't provide**: A 1GW data center running at 95% capacity factor needs ~8.3 TWh/year. Nuclear is the only zero-carbon source that matches that reliability profile. Tech hyperscalers are signing 20-year nuclear PPAs.
2. **Three Mile Island restart is the proof of concept**: Constellation restarted TMI Unit 1 in September 2024 — Microsoft signed a 20-year PPA. Every utility now has a business model for life extension and restart.
3. **Uranium supply is structurally short**: Kazakhstan's Kazatomprom cut 2024 guidance 14%. Primary mine supply has underinvested for a decade. Secondary inventory (from weapons downblend) is largely depleted. Cameco (CCJ) benefits most.
4. **SMR economics are improving**: NuScale VOYGR is NRC-certified. Oklo is partnered with Sam Altman. TerraPower (Gates), Kairos, X-energy are all in the pipeline. DOE loan guarantees de-risk first-of-a-kind construction cost.
5. **Bipartisan political support**: ADVANCE Act passed with 88-2 Senate vote. Expedited NRC licensing, export controls reform, fuel bank funding. Nuclear is the only energy source with genuine bipartisan support.
6. **Existing fleet is the safest bet**: CEG and VST own operating reactors with life extensions secured. Near-zero fuel cost, high capacity factors, 60-year asset lives. They are essentially long-duration bonds with equity upside from data center PPAs.

- Uranium oversupply: Kazakhstan reverses production cuts and new mines come online → CCJ thesis impaired
