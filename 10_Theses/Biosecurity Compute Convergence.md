- Bridge thesis linking [[Antimicrobial Resistance Pipeline]], [[Gene Editing CRISPR Therapeutics]], [[Alzheimers Disease Modification]], [[GLP-1 Metabolic Disease Revolution]], and the compute stack behind them.

## Core Logic
1. Clinical, omics, and platform-biotech workflows are absorbing more compute and automation every year.
2. If AI and high-performance compute shorten design, screening, and trial-selection cycles, biotech winners can emerge earlier than traditional valuation frameworks expect.
3. Infrastructure constraints, especially power and cloud capacity, can become an overlooked limiter on biotech platform velocity.
