## Thesis Statement
The US federal debt is $36T and climbing at $1T every 100 days. The structural fiscal deficit is 6–7% of GDP in peacetime — a level historically associated with currency debasement. Central banks globally are buying gold at the fastest rate since 1967 (1,037 tonnes in 2023, 1,045 tonnes in 2024). Bitcoin has a fixed supply of 21M and a growing institutional investor base following the ETF approval. Gold miners (Barrick, Newmont) are generating free cash flow at $2,000+ spot prices. This thesis does not require a crisis — it requires the slow math of debt monetization to continue, which it structurally must given political realities.

## Core Logic
1. **Fiscal dominance is the base case, not a tail risk**: With $36T in debt at ~4.5% average rate, interest expense exceeds $1T/year. This is larger than the defense budget. The Fed cannot raise rates aggressively without triggering a debt servicing crisis. Inflation is the path of least political resistance.
2. **Central bank gold buying is structural**: China, India, Poland, Turkey, and 30+ central banks are diversifying FX reserves away from US Treasuries. This is a slow regime shift — not a crisis — but it means gold has a sovereign floor bid that didn't exist pre-2022.
3. **Bitcoin ETF approval is the institutional on-ramp**: BlackRock IBIT and Fidelity FBTC have absorbed $50B+ in institutional capital in their first year. The institutional allocation is just beginning — 1% portfolio allocation from global wealth management would be $5T+ demand against 21M fixed supply.
4. **Gold miners have operating leverage to gold price**: At $1,800 gold, miners break even. At $2,300–2,500 gold, cash margins 2–3x. Wheaton Precious Metals (royalty model) has no mining cost exposure — pure price leverage with royalty income.
5. **Debasement is non-linear**: Gold and Bitcoin can underperform for years in Risk-On environments, then reprice violently during fiscal stress events. Holding 5–10% in hard money is portfolio insurance, not a trading position.
6. **MicroStrategy (MSTR) is a leveraged Bitcoin holding company**: MSTR has issued $7B+ in convertible notes to buy Bitcoin. It trades at a premium to NAV — essentially a leveraged Bitcoin ETF with equity characteristics. Higher risk/reward than direct Bitcoin exposure.
