## Core Logic
1. **Unmet need**: 280M+ people globally suffer from depression; ~30% are treatment-resistant
2. **Mechanism differentiation**: Psychedelic therapy acts on 5-HT2A receptors with rapid neuroplasticity effects — fundamentally different from SSRIs that take 4-6 weeks
3. **Single-dose economics**: One treatment session vs. daily pills reshapes payer economics and patient compliance
4. **FDA Breakthrough Therapy**: Accelerated review pathway reduces time-to-market risk
5. **Index inclusion catalyst**: CRSP/S&P inclusion (March 2026) triggers passive buying
