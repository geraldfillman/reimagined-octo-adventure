## Summary

- Nexis Uni is a practical library-access layer for paywalled news, legal commentary, and business reporting.
- It is most useful when the thesis needs reaction, commentary, or local reporting that does not surface in generic free news APIs.

## What It Provides

- Broad news and wire coverage
- Legal and business reporting
- Company and industry commentary
- Search and workspace tools for academic research

## Use Cases

- Reconstructing an event timeline around a thesis catalyst
- Checking how policy or regulation is being interpreted outside official releases
- Backfilling paywalled reporting when a signal needs better context

## Access Notes

- Usually available through university-library authentication.
