## Summary

- NAHB Wells Fargo Housing Market Index is the cleanest manual source for builder sentiment when a stable free API is not available.
- The headline release is public, timely, and widely cited across housing and homebuilder research.

## What It Provides

- Builder confidence index for single-family homebuilders
- Current sales conditions
- Expected sales over the next six months
- Traffic of prospective buyers

## Use Cases

- Confirming or challenging housing-thesis timing
- Tracking homebuilder sentiment ahead of permits, starts, and sales data
- Adding a qualitative demand read alongside rate and affordability indicators
