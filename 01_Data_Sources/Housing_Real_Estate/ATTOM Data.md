## Summary

- ATTOM provides property-level data including valuations, tax assessments, deed transfers, foreclosure filings, and natural hazard risk scores. Goes deeper than Zillow/Redfin aggregates — individual property intelligence.

## What It Provides

- Property valuations and AVM estimates
- Tax assessment history
- Deed transfer records
- Pre-foreclosure, auction, and REO listings
- Natural hazard risk scores (flood, fire, earthquake)
- School and neighborhood ratings

## Use Cases

- Property-level due diligence
- Foreclosure pipeline monitoring
- Climate risk overlay on housing portfolios
- Tax lien investment research
