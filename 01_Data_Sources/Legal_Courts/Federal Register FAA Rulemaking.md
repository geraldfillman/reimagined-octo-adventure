## Summary

- Federal Register FAA Rulemaking is the authoritative public timeline for FAA notices, proposed rules, final rules, and comment extensions.
- For the drone thesis, this is the cleanest way to track whether regulatory progress is real or just narrative.

## What It Provides

- Official text of FAA notices and rules
- Publication and effective dates
- Comment-period reopenings or extensions
- Public-inspection and recently published document feeds

## Use Cases

- Verifying whether an FAA milestone actually moved
- Tracking BVLOS, waiver, and integration-rule progress
- Building date-stamped regulatory evidence notes for thesis monitoring

## Integration Notes

**Status**: Not yet integrated
**API Key Required**: No
**Rate Limits**: None
**Update Frequency**: Daily / as published
**Data Format**: Web pages, XML, and linked official PDFs

## Access Notes

- Use the FAA agency page plus targeted searches for drone, BVLOS, waiver, and UAS terms.
- When the legal wording matters, verify against the linked official PDF or govinfo version.
