## Summary

- ProQuest Congressional is the strongest academic backfill for legislative and oversight context across defense, drones, fiscal policy, and regulatory questions.
- It is especially valuable when a thesis needs the committee-hearing or CRS-report layer that free sites rarely package well.

## What It Provides

- Hearings, reports, and committee prints
- Bills, resolutions, and legislative histories
- CRS reports and Congressional Record materials
- Presidential and regulatory supporting materials

## Use Cases

- Tracking defense-budget and drone-policy oversight
- Understanding how congressional language is shaping agency action
- Pulling historical hearing context into thesis reviews
