## Summary

- World Gold Council Goldhub is the most practical source for central-bank gold-reserve monitoring.
- It provides an easier research surface than raw IMF reserve tables while still grounding the dataset in official inputs.

## What It Provides

- Official gold reserve levels by country
- Monthly and annual changes in holdings
- Gold as a share of total reserves
- Downloadable reserve tables and methodology notes

## Use Cases

- Tracking hard-money accumulation by central banks
- Monitoring reserve behavior in the Dollar Debasement thesis
- Comparing reserve flows across major holders

## Integration Notes

**Status**: Not yet integrated
**API Key Required**: No
**Rate Limits**: None
**Update Frequency**: Monthly and quarterly
**Data Format**: Dashboard, downloadable spreadsheets, and methodology PDFs

## Access Notes

- Basic browsing is public; full filtering and downloads improve after free registration.
- The methodology explicitly references IMF IFS inputs, so this source pairs well with the IMF note for verification.
