# Pattern: Shell Entity Network

## Description

A web of related entities — subsidiaries, SPVs, holding companies, or affiliated funds — that obscures the true flow of money, liabilities, or control. The parent company presents a clean public face while material risk sits in off-balance-sheet structures.

## Signals

- Multiple subsidiaries with minimal disclosed revenue or operations
- Related-party transactions at non-arm's-length pricing
- Executives or directors appearing across multiple entities
- Shared addresses among "independent" entities
- Assets or liabilities that disappear between reporting periods
- Entities in secrecy jurisdictions (Cayman, BVI, Delaware LLC chains)
