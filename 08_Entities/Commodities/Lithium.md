## Overview
Critical battery metal for EVs and grid storage. Demand expected to grow 5-10x by 2030. Concentrated supply (Australia, Chile, China processing). Price extremely volatile — swung from $80k to $15k/tonne in 2022-2024.

## Supply/Demand Dynamics
- **Major Producers**: Australia, Chile, [[China]] (processing)
- **Major Consumers**: [[China]] (80% of battery production)

## Macro Sensitivity
- **EV Policy**: Government subsidies and mandates drive demand
- **China**: Dominates the supply chain
- **Technology**: Solid-state batteries could shift demand dynamics
