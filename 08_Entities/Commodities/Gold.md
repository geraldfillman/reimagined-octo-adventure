## Overview
The ultimate safe haven and inflation hedge. Inversely correlated with real yields (10Y Treasury minus inflation expectations) and [[DXY]]. Central bank reserve diversification is a structural tailwind.

## Supply/Demand Dynamics
- **Major Producers**: China, Australia, Russia
- **Major Consumers**: [[China]] (central bank + jewelry), [[India]] (jewelry)

## Macro Sensitivity
- **Inflation**: Gold rises when real rates are negative
- **USD Strength**: Strong [[DXY]] headwind (priced in USD)
- **Global Growth**: Irrelevant — gold is a fear/uncertainty play
