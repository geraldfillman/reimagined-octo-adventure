## Overview
Hybrid precious/industrial metal. ~50% industrial demand (electronics, solar panels) and ~50% investment/jewelry. More volatile than gold. Gold/silver ratio is a key relative value signal.

## Macro Sensitivity
- **Inflation**: Benefits like [[Gold]] but with industrial upside
- **Industrial Demand**: Solar panel growth is a structural tailwind
- **USD Strength**: Inversely correlated with [[DXY]]
