## Overview
World's largest publicly traded oil & gas company. Integrated — upstream (exploration/production), downstream (refining), and chemicals. Massive Permian Basin and Guyana assets. Strong balance sheet and dividend.

## Sector & Macro Exposure
- **Sector**: [[Energy]]
- **Country**: [[USA]] (HQ), global operations
- **Key Commodities**: [[Oil]] (primary driver), [[Natural Gas]]
- **Currency Exposure**: [[DXY]] — oil priced in USD, strong dollar headwind for non-US demand

## Bullish Factors
- [[Oil]] supply discipline (OPEC+ cuts)
- Guyana production ramp (low breakeven)
- [[Stagflation]] or [[Inflationary Boom]] environments

## Bearish Factors
- [[Recession]] destroys oil demand
- Long-term [[Energy Transition]] thesis
- Permian Basin depletion rates

- **Rising Rates**: Neutral — often hiking because energy is causing inflation
