## Overview
Alto Neuroscience is a precision psychiatry company using neuroscience biomarkers to match patients to targeted treatments for depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. Differentiated approach — biomarker-guided patient selection may reduce trial failure risk. Cash runway to 2028.

**Warning**: ALTO-100 MDD failure (Oct 2024) caused shares to drop ~70% in one day. Securities class action lawsuit filed.
