## Overview
Bright Minds Biosciences develops next-gen selective 5-HT2 serotonin receptor agonists designed to retain therapeutic benefits of psychedelics while eliminating hallucinogenic effects. "Psychedelic without the trip" thesis. Dual-listed on NASDAQ and CSE (Canada). Rare disease focus offers orphan drug potential.

## Pipeline
| Program | Mechanism | Indication | Stage |
|---------|-----------|------------|-------|
| BMB-101 | 5-HT2C agonist | Epileptic encephalopathies | Phase 2 |
| BMB-105 | 5-HT2C agonist | Prader-Willi Syndrome | Preclinical |
| BMB-201/202 | 5-HT2 agonists | Neuropsychiatric | Early |

## Bullish Factors
- Non-hallucinogenic mechanism avoids REMS complexity of classic psychedelics
- Rare disease focus = orphan drug designation potential + smaller trials
- Selective 5-HT2 targeting may offer cleaner safety profile
- Dual exchange listing broadens investor access

## Bearish Factors
- "Psychedelic without the trip" thesis is scientifically unproven at scale
- BMB-101 Phase 2 epilepsy is a binary event
- Thin pipeline; no revenue
