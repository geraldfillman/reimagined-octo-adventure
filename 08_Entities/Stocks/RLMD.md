## Overview
Relmada Therapeutics was a CNS-focused biotech developing esmethadone (REL-1017) for MDD. After three consecutive Phase 3 failures, the company has pivoted to oncology with NDV-01 for bladder cancer. **Note: Now fundamentally an oncology play — ETF inclusion may be a legacy classification.**

## Pipeline
| Program | Indication | Stage | Status |
|---------|------------|-------|--------|
| REL-1017 (esmethadone) | MDD | Phase 3 | **DISCONTINUED** — 3 failures |
| NDV-01 | Bladder cancer (NMIBC) | Phase 2a → Phase 3 | 90% response rate; Phase 3 H1 2026 |
| Sepranolone | CNS | Early | Limited data |

## Bullish Factors
- NDV-01 showed 90% response rate in bladder cancer Phase 2a
- Mizuho raised target to $19 (Outperform)
- Low market cap creates asymmetric upside if oncology asset succeeds

## Bearish Factors
- Core CNS thesis completely collapsed (3 Phase 3 failures)
- Company is now an oncology play misaligned with psychedelics ETF thesis
- Speculative with no commercial products
