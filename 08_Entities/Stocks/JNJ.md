## Overview

Q4 2025 sales: $503M, putting Spravato on a **$2B+ annualized run rate**. J&J guides $3-3.5B Spravato revenue by 2027-2028.

**Spravato is the single most important commercial validation signal for the entire psychedelic/mental health drug market.**
