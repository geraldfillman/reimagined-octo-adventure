## Overview
A weighted index of the US dollar against a basket of 6 major currencies (EUR, JPY, GBP, CAD, SEK, CHF). Strong DXY is bearish for commodities (priced in USD) and for US multinationals (revenue translation headwind).

## Impact Chain
1. DXY rising → [[Gold]] bearish, [[Oil]] bearish, emerging markets stressed
2. DXY falling → Commodities rally, US exporters benefit
3. Strong DXY → [[Risk-Off]] often accompanies flight to USD safety
