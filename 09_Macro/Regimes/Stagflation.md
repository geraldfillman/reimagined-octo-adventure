## Definition
Simultaneous stagnant economic growth and elevated inflation — the worst of both worlds. Central banks are trapped: raising rates worsens growth, cutting rates worsens inflation.

## Sector Impact Map
### Favored
- [[Energy]] — commodity prices elevated
- [[Materials]] — pricing power with real assets
- [[Consumer Staples]] — defensive + can pass through costs

### Challenged
- [[Tech Sector]] — high rates + slowing growth double hit
- [[Consumer Discretionary]] — squeezed consumer
- [[Financials]] — bad loans + flat curve

## Historical Precedents
- 1970s oil shock stagflation
- 2022: Stagflation fears (ultimately avoided)

## Current Assessment
Monitor for divergence between inflation and growth indicators.
