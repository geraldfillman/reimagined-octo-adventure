## Definition
Strong economic growth accompanied by rising inflation. Demand is outstripping supply. Corporate revenues grow but input costs rise. Eventually forces central bank tightening which transitions to Rate Hike Cycle.

## Sector Impact Map
### Favored
- [[Energy]] — demand-driven commodity price surge
- [[Materials]] — pricing power with scarce resources
- [[Industrials]] — riding the boom
- [[Financials]] — rising rates, strong loan demand

### Challenged
- [[Utilities]] — real yields turn positive, bond proxies lose appeal
- [[Consumer Staples]] — input cost pressure without pricing power
